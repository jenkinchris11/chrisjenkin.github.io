Heuristic Results
Question 1 - All users could exit the game at any time.

Question 2 - Most of the users took about 1 to 3 minutes to get used to the UI of the game.

Question 3 - User 1 found that it was easy to navigate as the controls were shown in the tutorial, User 2 had no problem navigating around the game, User 3 found no problem navigating the game, User 3 also found that using the mouse to aim was enjoyable.

Question 4 - User 1 said that there was no story tied into gameplay that he could see, User 2 said there was hints at some story butn nothing major, User 3 said that there was no story connected to gameplay.

Question 5 - User 1 said that there was some instruction in the tutorial level but not much further, User 2 said that there was no instrustions or a controller layout only small hints at what teh player was to do, User 3 said that the player only gets told where to go in the tutorial.

Survey Results
Question 1 - most people said that the UI was easy to see.

Question 2 - 8 out of 10 people said that they would change the way the health bar looks.

Question 3 - 9 out of 10 people agreed that the health icon is too big and that they would make it smaller.

Question 4 - 6 people thought that the pause menu was too small and 4 people thought that it was ok.

Question 5 - the votes were split on if the main menu was too big.
